# Sezonly — Seasonal Jobs Marketplace

Sezonly is a mobile-first marketplace for seasonal jobs in the Greek islands, connecting young workers with tourism businesses such as hotels, restaurants, cafés and beach bars.

**Live website:** https://www.sezonly.gr/

## Overview

Sezonly was created to make seasonal job searching in Greece more organized, transparent and efficient.

Instead of relying only on Facebook groups, scattered job posts and private messages, workers can browse seasonal job listings, view key job details and apply through a structured flow.

For employers, Sezonly provides a simple dashboard to publish job listings, manage applications and communicate with candidates more efficiently.

## Problem

Seasonal job search in Greece is often fragmented and chaotic:

- Job posts are spread across Facebook groups, generic job boards and personal messages
- Workers struggle to compare offers and understand job terms clearly
- Employers receive unstructured messages and applications from many different channels
- Important details like accommodation, food, salary and availability are often unclear

## Solution

Sezonly centralizes seasonal job listings and creates a clearer application experience for both sides of the marketplace.

The platform focuses on speed, simplicity and trust, especially for young people looking for summer work in Greek islands.

## Key Features
